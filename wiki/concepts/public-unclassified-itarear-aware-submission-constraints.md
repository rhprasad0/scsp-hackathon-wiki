# Public, unclassified, ITAR/EAR-aware submission constraints

Public, unclassified, ITAR/EAR-aware submission constraints are the submission rules that keep hackathon work in the public-safe, shareable, and unclassified lane. In the GenAI.mil challenge context, teams are explicitly told to submit only public, unclassified, or otherwise shareable data, with no classified materials and no controlled or restricted content. The guidance also says to mind ITAR and EAR, and to ensure any preexisting code has a determined export classification. ^[2026-04-25-genai-mil-challenge-prompt-and-rubric.md]

These constraints shape what kinds of projects are appropriate: the challenge is aimed at back-office, administrative, and logistics workflows, not tactical or kinetic AI. The source material flags that attempts to move into tactical, weapons-relevant, or other restricted domains are off-track and may also collide with ITAR/EAR rules and the unclassified-only requirement. ^[2026-04-25-genai-mil-challenge-prompt-and-rubric.md]

In practical terms, the safe design space is built around public DoD text and other approved public sources, with retrieval-grounded answers, structured drafting, and reviewable outputs. The organizer materials specifically point teams toward public datasets and APIs such as Army Publishing Directorate, Joint Travel Regulations, GSA Open APIs, SAM.gov, USAspending.gov, DTIC Public STINET, Federal Register, and eCFR bulk data. Related concepts include [[Controlled Reference Retrieval]], [[AI-assisted spec with human review]], and [[Back-office military workflow use cases]]. ^[2026-04-25-genai-mil-challenge-prompt-and-rubric.md]

The same source material emphasizes that public submissions must remain public throughout the hackathon and beyond, and that the README should document the team, track, build, data sources, and run instructions. It also requires at least one teammate to demo in person, with a short presentation window, and notes that SCSP will publish all submissions on its GitHub. ^[2026-04-25-genai-mil-challenge-prompt-and-rubric.md]
